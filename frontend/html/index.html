❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about how BarterLearn works? We have answers.

How do Time Credits work?

Time Credits are the platform's internal currency. One hour of teaching earns you exactly one Time Credit. You can then use this Time Credit to buy one hour of learning from anyone else on the platform, regardless of the subject.

Do I have to teach the same person who teaches me?

No! That's the beauty of BarterLearn. We support indirect bartering. You can teach JavaScript to User A, earn a Time Credit, and use that credit to learn Spanish from User B. You don't have to swap directly with the same person.

Is there a membership fee to join?

Joining BarterLearn is completely free! Everyone starts with 1 free Time Credit to test the waters and schedule their first session. After that, you earn credits by sharing your knowledge with others.

What if a session doesn't go well or is cancelled?

We have a secure dispute system. If a tutor cancels last minute or doesn't show up, your Time Credit is refunded to your wallet. You can rate and review every session, which helps us keep our community standards high.
